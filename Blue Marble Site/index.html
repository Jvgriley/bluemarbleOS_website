Jack Riley

CEO & Founder

Jack Riley founded Blue Marble with a simple belief: the more clearly we understand our planet, the better equipped we are to protect it. Driven by a passion for conservation and technology, he created Blue Marble to bridge the gap between environmental intelligence and real-world action.

Taking a hands-on, boots-on-the-ground approach, Jack works closely with existing conservation organisations while building new partnerships that deliver meaningful, measurable impact. His vision is to create a global platform that empowers those already protecting wildlife and ecosystems with the tools, data and support needed to scale their mission.
